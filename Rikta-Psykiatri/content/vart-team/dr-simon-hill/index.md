Dr Simon Hill is a consultant psychiatrist whose career spans specialist neuropsychiatry services, memory clinics, and community mental health teams. He has led multidisciplinary services supporting adults with ADHD alongside cognitive or neurological conditions.

At Rikta Psychiatry, Dr Hill focuses on comprehensive assessments for patients whose presentations include executive dysfunction, memory concerns, or subtle cognitive changes. He is known for synthesising neuroimaging, neuropsychology reports, and lived experience into plans that feel both precise and human.

Dr Hill collaborates closely with Rikta’s psychologists, nurses, and occupational therapists to ensure continuity from diagnosis through long-term follow-up and shared-care arrangements.
