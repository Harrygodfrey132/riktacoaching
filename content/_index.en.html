The process

How our coaching works

We start by mapping your daily challenges, goals, and the pace that fits your life. You get practical tools, homework, and between-session support so the change lasts.

  1. Initial conversation

    We map your goals, daily challenges, and the pace that fits your life.

  2. Personalised coaching plan

    Your coach builds a plan with tools, routines, and clear focus areas.

  3. Regular structured sessions

    We meet consistently to practice skills and adjust what works.

  4. Ongoing support and adjustments

    Homework, check-ins, and monthly reviews keep progress moving.
