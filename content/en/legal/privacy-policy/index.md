Rikta Psychiatry (Riktningskliniken AB) respects your privacy and handles personal data with confidentiality and care. This policy explains what data we collect, why we use it, and the choices and rights you have, including data collected through Facebook ads.

## Who is responsible for your data
Riktningskliniken AB is the data controller for personal data processed through our website and services.

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at `info@riktapsykiatri.se`.

## What data we collect
We may collect and process:
- Contact details you provide (name, email, phone).
- Information you submit via forms or during care, such as your reason for contact.
- Technical data from your visit to the website (e.g., IP address, device details, and usage analytics), including information from Facebook ads.

## Why we use your data
We use personal data to:
- Respond to inquiries and provide care-related communication.
- Deliver and improve our services.
- Meet legal and regulatory obligations related to healthcare.
- Ensure security, quality, and documentation in our clinical processes.

## Legal basis
We process personal data based on one or more of the following:
- Consent (for certain communications or when you choose to submit forms).
- Contract or steps before entering a contract.
- Legal obligations under healthcare and data protection laws.
- Legitimate interests for service quality and security.

## Where data is stored
We store submitted information in Kaddio.

## How we protect your data
We apply appropriate organizational and technical safeguards, including access control and confidentiality requirements for staff and partners involved in providing care.

## Sharing and processors
We only share personal data when necessary to provide services or comply with law. We may use trusted service providers (data processors) who act on our instructions and under written agreements.

## How long we keep data
We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and as required by applicable healthcare and data protection laws.

## Your rights
Under GDPR, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data.
- Request correction of inaccurate information.
- Request deletion in certain cases.
- Object to certain processing or request restriction.
- Request data portability where applicable.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at `info@riktapsykiatri.se`.

## Complaints
If you have concerns about how we handle personal data, you can contact us first. You may also file a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (IMY).

## Updates to this policy
We may update this policy as our services or regulations change. The latest version will always be published on this page.
