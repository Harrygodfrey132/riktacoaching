Access to Work

Access to Work can fund ADHD coaching.

Access to Work is a UK scheme that can cover specialist ADHD coaching, plus equipment or travel support, so you can work with fewer barriers.

Who can apply

  • 16+ and living in England, Scotland, or Wales
  • In paid work, self‑employed, or starting a new role
  • Your condition affects work or commuting

What it can fund

  • Specialist ADHD coaching (as part of your award)
  • Other support workers where appropriate
  • Assistive or specialist equipment
  • Travel support

What you’ll need

  • Your role details and employment status
  • How ADHD affects your workday
  • A workplace contact (if applicable)

Common support ideas

  • Noise‑cancelling headphones
  • Visual timers and reminders
  • Kanban tools or planners
  • Sit‑stand desk or alternative seating

What happens next?

An assessor will discuss your role and needs, then outline the support available. You’ll get an estimated timeline during your application.
