<section class="coachning-section" id="value">
  <div class="coachning-panel">
    <h2>What this coaching helps you solve</h2>
    <p>
      ADHD coaching is not about generic advice. It is about building a system that works with your brain in real life.
      We focus on problems that affect everyday functioning.
    </p>
    <ul class="coachning-list">
      <li><strong>Task initiation:</strong> Start important tasks without waiting for last-minute pressure.</li>
      <li><strong>Concentration:</strong> Improve focus with routines that reduce distraction and mental switching.</li>
      <li><strong>Planning and deadlines:</strong> Turn large goals into small, realistic actions.</li>
      <li><strong>Procrastination:</strong> Use practical anti-avoidance tools that are easy to repeat.</li>
      <li><strong>Emotional regulation:</strong> Reduce overwhelm, impulsive reactions, and shutdown patterns.</li>
      <li><strong>Consistency:</strong> Keep progress week to week, not only on good days.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
