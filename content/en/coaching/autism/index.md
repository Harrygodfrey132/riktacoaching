  <div class="coachning-panel">
    <h2>What we work on</h2>
    <p>
      Autism affects how the brain filters input and how routines and relationships feel. Together we map your strengths
      and challenges and design systems that make everyday life more predictable.
    </p>
    <ul class="coachning-list">
      <li><strong>Sensory balance:</strong> Strategies to reduce overwhelm and plan recovery.</li>
      <li><strong>Communication:</strong> Support for expressing needs and understanding social cues.</li>
      <li><strong>Planning:</strong> Methods for handling transitions, change, and decisions.</li>
      <li><strong>Relationships:</strong> Tools for building safety with family, friends, and at work.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
