Rikta Psychiatry (Riktningskliniken AB) respects your privacy and handles personal data with confidentiality and care. This policy explains what data we collect, why we use it, and the choices and rights you have, including data collected through Facebook ads.

## Who is responsible for your data
Riktningskliniken AB is the data controller for personal data processed through our website and services.

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at `info@riktapsykiatri.se`.

## What data we collect
We may collect and process:
- Contact details you provide (name, email, phone).
- Information you submit via forms or during care, such as your reason for contact.
- Technical data from your visit to the website (e.g., IP address, device details, and usage analytics), including information from Facebook ads.

## Why we use your data
We use personal data to:
- Respond to inquiries and provide care-related communication.
- Deliver and improve our services.
- Meet legal and regulatory obligations related to healthcare.
- Ensure security, quality, and documentation in our clinical processes.
