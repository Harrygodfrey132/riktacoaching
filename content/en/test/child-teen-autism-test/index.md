      <aside class="adhd-screening__note">
        <h3>Scoring and use</h3>
        <p><strong>Scoring:</strong> Only 1 point can be scored for each question. Score 1 point for Definitely or Slightly Agree on each of items 1, 5, 7 and 10. Score 1 point for Definitely or Slightly Disagree on each of items 2, 3, 4, 6, 8 and 9. If the individual scores 6 or above, consider referring them for a formal diagnostic assessment.</p>
        <p><strong>Use:</strong> This is the child version of the test recommended in the NICE clinical guideline CG142.</p>
        <p><strong>Key reference:</strong> Allison C, Auyeung B, and Baron-Cohen S. (2012) Journal of the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry 51(2):202-12.</p>
