Dr Chris Kalafatis is a consultant psychiatrist recognised for his work on complex mood disorders and the neuropsychiatric interface. He supports patients who need nuanced medication plans, collaborative titration for ADHD, or second opinions following incomplete assessments.

Chris has held academic and clinical appointments across the NHS and King’s College London, contributing to research on mood stabilisation and cognition. At Rikta Psychiatry he leads case conferences for presentations that blend affective, cognitive, and neurodevelopmental features—ensuring recommendations account for the whole clinical picture.

His consultations balance precision and empathy: patients leave with a structured plan, clear safety guidance, and a sense of partnership in decision-making.
