Dr Sourabh Singh is a consultant psychiatrist with over 15 years of experience delivering structured ADHD and mood disorder assessments for adults. He combines NHS leadership experience with a calm, collaborative style that helps patients feel heard while translating complex findings into practical next steps.

At Rikta Psychiatry, Dr Singh guides multidisciplinary formulations for adults navigating overlapping ADHD, anxiety, and trauma presentations. He partners closely with therapists and nurses to ensure every care plan balances evidence-based interventions with day-to-day support. When helpful, he works with employers and academic institutions to make sure recommendations translate into real accommodations.

Outside direct consultations, Dr Singh mentors early-career clinicians and contributes to Rikta’s quality governance framework, drawing on his tenure as Associate Clinical Director at Oxleas NHS Foundation Trust.
