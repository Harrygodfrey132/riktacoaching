Dr Joanna Woodger is a consultant psychiatrist specialising in women’s mental health and neurodiversity across the adult lifespan. She is particularly experienced in assessing ADHD when symptoms have been masked for years by high achievement, burnout, or hormonal transitions.

During her NHS leadership roles, Dr Woodger modernised liaison psychiatry services and built collaborative pathways with primary care, obstetrics, and perinatal teams. At Rikta Psychiatry she applies that same systems thinking to help patients navigate multi-service support—clarifying diagnoses, aligning medication decisions, and planning stepwise therapeutic input.

Patients describe Dr Woodger’s consultations as clear, validating, and thorough. She makes space for the emotional impact of late diagnosis while grounding treatment decisions in evidence and shared decision-making.
