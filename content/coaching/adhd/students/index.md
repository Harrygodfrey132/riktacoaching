<section class="coachning-section" id="why-students-struggle">
  <div class="coachning-panel">
    <h2>Why ADHD can hit hard in education</h2>
    <p>
      Education expects constant self-management. ADHD often makes the hidden workload much heavier.
    </p>
    <ul class="coachning-list">
      <li><strong>Deadline blindness:</strong> Work can feel far away until it becomes urgent all at once.</li>
      <li><strong>Task initiation:</strong> Starting coursework can feel disproportionately hard, even when you care.</li>
      <li><strong>Overload cycles:</strong> Too many open loops can lead to shutdown and avoidance.</li>
      <li><strong>Confidence drop:</strong> Repeated near-misses can quickly turn into stress and self-doubt.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
