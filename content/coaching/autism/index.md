<section class="coachning-section" id="themes">
  <div class="coachning-panel">
    <h2>What this coaching helps you solve</h2>
    <p>
      Autism coaching focuses on reducing overload and improving daily function in ways that feel sustainable.
      We work with your strengths while lowering friction in everyday situations.
    </p>
    <ul class="coachning-list">
      <li><strong>Sensory overload:</strong> Build practical strategies for noise, crowds, transitions, and recovery.</li>
      <li><strong>Executive function:</strong> Create simple systems for planning, prioritising, and follow-through.</li>
      <li><strong>Communication strain:</strong> Improve clarity, boundaries, and confidence in conversations.</li>
      <li><strong>Social fatigue:</strong> Plan social demands so energy is protected and burnout risk is lower.</li>
      <li><strong>Routine instability:</strong> Design flexible routines that survive real-life disruptions.</li>
      <li><strong>Stress and shutdown patterns:</strong> Use early-warning and reset plans to recover faster.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</section>
